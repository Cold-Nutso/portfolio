Small Art

Logos, icons, and pictographs make up a significant portion of my digital art. I like my work minimal and symbolic. Shapes matter most, so ideally the piece should work even if monochromatic. I try to find the simplest form I can to represent an idea.

Click here for all my small art!

Big Art

Sometimes I make art that's a little more visually complex than your average symbol. It's usually a character, because they're the most fun. I'm still reasonably new to digital drawing so I'm frequently changing styles and Photoshop brushes.

Click here for all my big art!

Pixel Art

This is the first medium I started working to improve in and it is very dear to my heart. Pixel art is a more limited format than pencil drawing, so your choices have a lot of weight to them. You can also make a crochet project out of any of these pretty easily.

You can click this, but I haven't uploaded everything.

Digital Sketches

The majority of the art I do is drawn in a spiral notebook. Blank paper is too intimidating, so all my physical drawing has to be done on lined paper. Problem is, that doesn't scan very well so I've been trying to do more sketches on the computer.
