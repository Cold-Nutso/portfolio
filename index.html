Small Art

Logos, icons, and pictographs make up a significant portion of my digital art. I like my work minimal and symbolic. Shapes matter most, so ideally the piece should work even if monochromatic. I try to find the simplest form I can to represent an idea.

Big Art

This is the first medium I started working to improve in and it is very dear to my heart. Pixel art is a more limited format than pencil drawing, so your choices have a lot of weight to them. You can also make a crochet project out of any of these pretty easily.
