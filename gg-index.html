What is GardenGrave?

Long ago, the lives of nearly every organism on Earth were grinded to a halt through the catastrophic assaults of World War IV. Ever since, life has been steadily recovering from the fallout. Societies are advancing and now we face a world reborn with new species and customs, all built from the few remnants of the past they could find.

Patron Hue

All functions involved in operating reality are divided among six total patron hues, each with a unique range of responsibilities and exploits. Since the Perforation, there have been many documented cases of organisms born with a much stronger connection to any given patron hue. This is generally known as the angel phenomenon. A further subset, classified as the labor angels, is made up of hosts that seem to share a consistently reoccuring power. Only two such lines have been documented.

The Frijid

Six angels travel together across Frijfeld, making the best of their isolated lives.
